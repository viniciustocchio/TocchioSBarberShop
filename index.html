About

Inspired by old New York barbershops from the 40s, 50s, and 60s, Tocchio's Barbershop focuses on bringing back the male culture lost over the years, in which men met to shave with a razor and cut their hair while they drink, talk and chill.


Between warm towels and facial massage, the best creams and foams prepare our clients' faces. Also, our visitors can enjoy the space in our brewery.

Services

Services that go beyond beard, hair and mustache. Our services usually exceed expectations, with trained and updated professionals, highly capable of performing their tasks with excellence.
